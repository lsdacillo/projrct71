  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsobilities to consider, canteens must be better organized and more efficient than they have been in the past. Online menus with photographs or the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account n the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary equierments they have. Ordering online by tickiting a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss of money handling, making it safer and less complicated for staff and students alike.   • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them ith supporting computional devices. Automated attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the enevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data.
    2. Economics Have you ever thought that attendacnce management systems could controll costs? It saves money by putting an end to inacurate time reporting, buddy punching, attendance, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact. a study done by the Aesium Group found that large organizations save up to $1,600 per employee after implementing automated attendance management systems.
    3. Productivity/Efficiency Monetering and managing attendance manually can be time consuming, laborous, and expensive affair. It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards, create scheduals, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually. Free up precious administration time with an automated system that does it all for you, from keeping track of employee hours to automatically importing information into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optomizing the use of recources which lead to increased productivity and improved profits.
